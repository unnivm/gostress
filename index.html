Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What platforms are supported?

A: Linux, macOS, and Windows (amd64 and arm64)

Q: Does GoStress support POST requests?

A: Not yet, but support is planned for an upcoming release.

Q: Does GoStress support other protocols?

A: Not yet, GoStressonly supports only HTTP(s) protocols. But support for other protocols is planned for an upcoming release.
